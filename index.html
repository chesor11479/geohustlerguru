About Me

Geographic Information Systems (GIS): I specialize in GIS applications, utilizing spatial data to analyze and solve real-world problems. My expertise includes spatial analysis, mapping, and geostatistical modeling to support decision-making in environmental management, urban planning, and natural resource conservation.

Remote Sensing: With a strong background in remote sensing, I process and analyze satellite and aerial imagery to extract valuable insights on land cover changes, vegetation health, and climate impacts. Leveraging tools like Google Earth Engine and machine learning techniques, I develop predictive models for environmental monitoring and sustainable development.

Surveying: My experience in surveying involves precise data collection for topographic mapping, land parcel delineation, and infrastructure development. Using advanced tools such as LiDAR, GNSS, and UAV technology, I ensure accurate spatial data acquisition for various geospatial applications.
